
# Problem 4
$x_{1}=2x_{3}$
$x_{2}=4x_{4}$

Zero Vector: Yes
	$0=2(0)\to 0=0$
	$0=4(0)\to 0=0$
Mult:
	$v=\begin{bmatrix}x_{1}\\x_{2}\\x_{3}\\x_{4}\end{bmatrix}, \lambda V=\begin{bmatrix}\lambda x_{1}\\\lambda x_{2}\\\lambda x_{3}\\\lambda x_{4}\end{bmatrix}$
	$\lambda x_{1}=\lambda 3x_{3}=3(\lambda x_{3})$
	$\lambda x_{2}=\lambda_{4}x_{4}=4(\lambda x_{4})$
Addition:
	$v=\begin{bmatrix} x_{1}\\x_{2}\\x_{3}\\x_{4}\end{bmatrix},w=\begin{bmatrix} y_{1}\\y_{2}\\y_{3}\\y_{4}\end{bmatrix}$
	$x_{1}=3x_{3}, y_{1}=3y_{3}$
	$x_{2}=4x_{4}, y_{2}=4y_{2}$
	$v+w=\begin{bmatrix}x_{1}+y_{1}\\x_{2}+y_{2}\\x_{3}+y_{3}\\x_{4}+y_{4}\end{bmatrix}$
	$x_{1}+y_{1}=3x_{3}+3y_{3}=3(x_{3}+y_{3})$
	$x_{2}+y_{2}=4x_{4}+4x_{4}=4(x_{4}+y_{4})$
