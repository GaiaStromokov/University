# Common curves
a line starting at $(x_{0},y_{0},z_{0})$ and ending at $(x_{1},y_{1},z_{1})$
they can be represented by 
- $x=x_{0}+(x_{1}-x_{0})t$
- $y=y_{0}+(y_{1}-y_{0})t$
- $z=z_{0}+(z_{1}-z_{0})t$
___
